About us

The Humanity Urgent Action Fund (HUAF) is a not-for-profit initiative that combines policy solutions with immediate humanitarian action to address society’s urgent social challenges. The project focuses on marginalized minority groups such as women, young people and groups leaving in poverty.


Research shows that development institutional aid reaches rarely reaches the continents marginalized groups of women and those leaving in poverty. While there are many challenges in sustainably meeting the needs of rural low-income communities through aid, overcoming these challenges has been a focus of HUAF towards providing access to opportunities.

  • Economic Justice

    The unequal distribution of income and opportunity between different groups in society erodes dignity from the poor and contribute to human insecurity. It is a concern in almost all countries around the world and often people are trapped in poverty with little chance to climb up the social ladder. Our work to end economic injustice calls for a combination of policy solutions and humanitarian action.

  • Protecting Women and Girl’s Dignity

    We believe every woman and girl has the right to live of dignity, without fear, coercion, violence and discrimination. Every woman has the right to health and protection from sexual violence. Violence against women is not inevitable. We are actively working towards building a future in which families and communities can change social norms and attitudes.
